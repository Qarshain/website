Frequently Asked Questions

How is Garshain Shariah-compliant?
Garshain uses Murabaha and Qard Hasan structures for its financing products. All contracts and processes are reviewed and approved by our Shariah Board, consisting of respected scholars in Islamic finance.
Who can apply for a loan?
Saudi citizens and legal residents (with valid Iqama) aged 21 and above with a regular income source can apply for loans on Garshain. We welcome employees, business owners, and freelancers.
How quickly can I receive funds?
After approval, funds are typically disbursed within 24 hours to your registered bank account. The approval process usually takes 1-2 business days for first-time borrowers and can be as quick as a few hours for returning customers with good standing.
How do I repay my loan?
Repayments are made through automatic monthly deductions from your registered bank account or SADAD payments. You can also make early repayments through our app or website without any penalties.
